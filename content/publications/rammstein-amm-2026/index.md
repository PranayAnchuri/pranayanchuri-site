abstract: >
  We address the problem of optimal liquidity management in concentrated automated market
  makers (AMMs) using optimal control theory. RAmmStein formulates the rebalancing decision
  as a Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman quasi-variational inequality and solves it via deep
  reinforcement learning, incorporating mean-reversion dynamics to learn when to rebalance.
  Evaluated on 6.8M Coinbase trades, the approach achieves 1.60% net ROI while reducing
  rebalancing frequency by 85% compared to greedy strategies.
